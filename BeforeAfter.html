Diabetic foot ulcer

Offloading plus NPWT closed a plantar ulcer

A patient with neuropathy presented with a deep plantar ulcer. Surgical debridement, negative pressure, and weekly advanced dressings reduced infection risk and supported granulation until the wound filled in.
