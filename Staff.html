Our Staff

Meet the clinicians who will partner with you on your wound healing journey.

Dr. Quyen T. Ha, M.D., CWSP

Certified Wound Specialist Physician · Founder, River Valley Wound Care

Dr. Quyen T. Ha is a board-certified physician and Certified Wound Specialist Physician (CWSP) with advanced expertise in the treatment of complex and non-healing wounds. As the founder of River Valley Wound Care, he brings decades of clinical experience and a deeply patient-centered philosophy.

Dr. Ha is also the author of A Runaway Teenager Who Found Freedom, which tells his remarkable journey from a teenager fleeing Vietnam to becoming a wound care physician in the United States. His lived experience gives him a unique empathy for patients and a commitment to clear communication, dignity, and trust.

His clinical interests include diabetic foot ulcers, venous and arterial ulcers, surgical wounds, limb preservation, and the use of advanced wound technologies such as negative pressure therapy and cellular tissue products.

Paul W. Dobbs, APRN

Nurse Practitioner

Paul Dobbs is an experienced Advanced Practice Registered Nurse with a strong background in wound management and primary care. He is known for his calm bedside manner, excellent communication skills, and team-focused approach to patient care.

Paul works closely with patients and families to explain treatment options, monitor healing progress, and coordinate care with referring providers.

Bobbi J. Dorey, LPN

Licensed Practical Nurse

Bobbi Dorey is a highly skilled Licensed Practical Nurse with extensive experience in wound assessment, dressing changes, and treatment support.

Her friendly demeanor and attention to detail help create a welcoming and efficient clinic experience while ensuring that dressings, supplies, and follow-up care stay on track.
